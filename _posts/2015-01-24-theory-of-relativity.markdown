If the title seems too scientific for you, don't worry! There's nothing scientific here. But still, you will have to deduce theories and outcomes. This is just a couple of case studies.

**1**

Scene 1:

After a few years long search, a girl's marriage has been fixed by her family. Everybody is happy. Girl likes the boy and boy likes the girl.

Scene 2:

A relative (who happened to be a close relative) comes and tells the family that the guy is not good and we should not conduct this marriage. Family agrees. The girl is left with no choice but to agree.

**2**

Scene 1:

A girl's marriage has been fixed by her family. Everybody seems to be happy. The engagement ceremony happens.

Scene 2:

The girl says that the guy misbehaves to her. The family and relatives decide that this marriage is not suitable for her and they drop it.

Scene 3:

The girl marries her boyfriend. But, here is the twist in the story: The bad behaviour of the boy turned out to be a cooked up story by the girl's family, since she's in love with somebody else.

---

Someone or a group of someones suck(s) in both the cases.
